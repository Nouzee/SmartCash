# Require identity confirmation for the smart-money strategy

Candidate-shock discovery and the `MicrostructureConfirmed` benchmark use price, book, and aggressor flow without requiring identity mapping. Promotion to `SmartMoneyConfirmed`, and therefore eligibility for the headline smart-money strategy, additionally requires sufficient as-of seat/broker-entity coverage and aligned identity evidence such as net flow, concentration, or a matured historical skill prior. Missing identity evidence is `identity_unavailable`, never an opposing or zero-valued signal.

## Consequences

- Research reports compare an identity-free microstructure benchmark, an identity overlay, and the full promoted strategy.
- Identity coverage gates and historical-prior maturity are reported with every promoted event.
- An identity-unavailable candidate may remain in benchmark evaluation but cannot enter headline smart-money performance.
- Identity conflict is reserved for sufficiently covered, explicitly opposing mapped flow.
